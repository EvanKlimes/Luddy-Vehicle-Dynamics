Overview / Story

Why slip angle matters

This dashboard explains visually why a slip angle sensor helps Model Predictive Control (MPC), state estimation, and tire parameter identification. MPC relies on physics models; the single-track (bicycle) dynamic model needs lateral velocity (vy) and slip to predict forces. A direct slip measurement reduces vy uncertainty and improves tire model fits (Pacejka-style).
